Wellcome to Murree Most Famous Places

Murree, once a charming colonial town nestled in the Himalayan foothills, is now a popular hill station and bustling summer resort in the Galyat region of northern Punjab. Due to being a picturesque and pleasantly scenic places in the country, the last one decade have seen rampant development and today Murree more closely resembles a litter-strewn overcrowded and a thriving tourist resort. However it remains exceedingly popular with Pakistani families for whom its truly a tourist paradise and receives glowing recommendations from this demographic. In summer it is cool - even chilly in the evening - while light rain is common. In winter Murree hides herself under thick blanket of snow. It is the birthplace of explorer Francis Younghusband.

Nathiagali

Nathia Gali is a charming hill station in Pakistan. Nathia Gali is located in Abbottabad district of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Nathia Gali has hilly mountains, lush green meadows, beautiful cottages, and lovely cherry blossom trees. Nathia Gali is surrounded by walnut, maple and oak trees. Nathia Gali is the famous mountain resort in Pakistan. You can easily reach to Nathia Gali from Islamabad in two hours. Tourists can enjoy hiking, horse riding, skiing and climbing in Nathia Gali. May, June, July, and August are the best months for climbing. The main beauty of Nathia Gali is greenery. In snowfall, Nathia Gali looks more ravishing and attractive. Nathia Gali is the best place to enjoy snowfall. In Nathia Gali there are many small and large hotels, Government rest houses for tourists.

It is prominent for its unique flowers and animals, which are not found anywhere else in Pakistan.
